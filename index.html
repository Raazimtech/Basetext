What is BaseText?

BaseText is an AI-powered study assistant that helps students and professionals generate chapter summaries, flashcards, practice exams, and study notes from PDF, DOCX, PPTX, and text files. The platform works entirely locally, ensuring your documents remain secure while supporting efficient exam preparation without requiring cloud processing.

Key Features

  • AI Summary Generator: Automatically extract key terminology and executive summaries from long documents.
  • Automatic Flashcard Creation: Convert dense material into interactive study cards.
  • Mock Exam Generation: Auto-generate multiple-choice questions to test your knowledge.
  • PPTX & DOCX Support: Extract text seamlessly from slide decks and Word files. PPTX files are automatically converted to clean documents.
  • Offline Study Workspace: Save and review your generated study materials securely in your browser.
